President Numayri will become particularly concerned if the fighting spreads to the Chad-Sudan border area, an area of chronic Sudanese dissidence. Numayri fears that Libya's support for the Chadian rebels is inspired by the Soviets and part of a broader plan to undermine Western-oriented governments in the region. The Sudanese believe the Soviets want to overthrow Numayri to strike a blow at Egypt. The Sudanese are already in something of a bind on policy toward the Horn of Africa. Saudi Arabia, which provides Sudan with substantial economic support, is urging Numayri to back the Somalis against Ethiopia.
