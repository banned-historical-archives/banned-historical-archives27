His close political lackeys have appeared at the 22nd congress in important roles for no other reason than their personal association with Khrushchev-- much in the manner of Stalin's appointment of his own palace guard to the central committee. As leader of the Communist party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev has succeeded in molding the party, its policies, and its intended course in his own image.
