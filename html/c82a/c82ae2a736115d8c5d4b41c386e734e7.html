It is intended to alert government analysts and policymakers concerned with terrorism to the potential for political violence as these dates approach. Certain holidays and anniversaries hold special significance for some terrorist groups-especially those with an ethnic base. Such a group would be likely to time a terrorist operation to coincide with one of these dates.
