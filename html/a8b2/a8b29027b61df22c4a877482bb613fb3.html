PALESTINIAN ISSUES Moscow continues its reticence in matters pertaining to the Palestinians. The visit of a Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) delegation to Moscow was not acknowledged until the release by TASS of a "communique for the press" on 26 November, two days after the visit ended. The communique referred to the Palestinians' legitimate "national" rights, as did the Soviet- Yugoslav communique on Tito's recent talks with Brezhnev in Kiev, but Moscow comment otherwise has generally avoided including the word "national."
