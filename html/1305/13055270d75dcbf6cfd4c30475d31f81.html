In its latest attack, which appeared in a widely publicized editorial in the 4 May Liberation Army Daily, Peking charged that "antiparty, antisocialist elements" have embarked on a new offensive. Such dissident attitudes will be of increasing concern as the war in Vietnam esca- lates and as the regime prepares for the departure of Mao Tse-tung from the scene.
