THE CPC NO LONGER PUBLICLY PROMOTES QIGONG ((SUBHEAD)) AS A MATTER OF FACT, AT THE BACK OF QIGONG FUNCTIONS LIES A WHOLE SET OF CULTURES TOTALLY DIFFERENT FROM COMMUNISM. THOSE PRACTICING QIGONG BELIEVE THAT THERE ARE GODS AND SPIRITS IN THE HEAVENS, AND THAT ALL PEOPLE HAVE SOULS. THEY ALSO BELIEVE THAT THE WORLD IS NEITHER PHYSICAL NOR CONCEPTUAL, BUT IS THE INDESCRIBABLE "WORD" ((DAO 6670)) AND ELEMENTS, OR THE "ELEMENTARY BREATH." TO THE MODERN MAN,. THEY ARE "NONEXISTENT," WHILE ON A CERTAIN LEVEL OF QIGONG THEY REALLY EXIST. ON THIS LEVEL, PEOPLE CAN DIRECTLY PERCEIVE THE WORLD AND SIMPLY AND ACCURATELY MASTER IT. HUMAN BIRTH, AGE,. ILLNESS, AND DEATH AND THE WORLD'S MEETING AND SEPARATION ALL HAVE BECOME SIMPLE AND UNDERSTANDABLE. ALL ARE INEVITABLE AND FATALISTIC, AND VOID AND EMPTY. THE CHINESE PEOPLE HAVE LOST CONFIDENCE IN THE CPC'S PROMISES, AND SO THEY USE "QIGONG" OR SIMILAR BELIEFS TO FILL THE VACUUM IN THEIR SOULS. THIS IS, INDEED, A GREAT SATIRE ON THE FUNDAMENTAL TENETS OF MARXISM. THE CPG TAKES A VERY UTILITARIAN AND PRACTICAL ATTITUDE TOWARD QIGONG. WITHIN THE SIX MONTHS BEGINNING FROM THE TIME BEFORE "4 JUNE" TO THE TIME AFTER IT, THE CPC ENCOURAGED AND SUPPORTED PEOPLE TO PRACTICE QIGONG. NEWSPAPERS AND MAGAZINES FREQUENTLY PROMOTED AND INTRODUCED QIGONG KNOWLEDGE, AND THE GOVERNMENT ALSO HELD SYMPOSIUMS ON QIGONG FREQUENTLY. BECAUSE AT THAT TIME "QIGONG" WAS NOT INVOLVED IN POLITICS. IT NEITHER. ATTEMPTED TO OVERTHROW THE CPC NOR DEMANDED IT TO SPEED UP REFORM IN ANY FORM. IN THESE CIRCUMSTANCES, THE QIGONG CRAZE SPREAD AS NEVER BEFORE. SOME INNER INFORMATION SPREAD THAT THEY KNEW BEFOREHAND THAT WITHIN A CERTAIN TIME THE CPC WOULD DEFINLTELY LOSE MORE OF THE PEOPLE'S SUPPORT, AND THAT "4 JUNE" WOULD BE "REDRESSED." IT ALSO DISCLOSED THE LIFESPANS OF THE POLITICAL OLD MEN OF THE CPC.
