The Chinese Communists continue to express displeasure over Hanoi's decision to enter talks with the US, but they have avoided any hints of reprisals, such as a reduction of their technical and material assistance to North Vietnam. Despite shrill demands in Peking's propaganda for the purge of additional "traitors" in the domestic political struggle, there apparently have been no attempts to bring down new victims since the dramatic ouster of acting chief of staff Yang Cheng-wu in March.
