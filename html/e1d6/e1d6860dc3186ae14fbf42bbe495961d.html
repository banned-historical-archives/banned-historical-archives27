Nearly 600 mortar shells were fired at US field positions in southwestern Kontum Province near the Cambodian border on 12 November. A Communist force--estimated at two battalions-- then attacked a three- company element of the ten- battalion US Operation PAUL REVERE IV. The Americans retaliated with artillery, air strikes, and ground attacks. Casualties during this encoun- ter, which continued through 13 November, have been light with 32 Communists killed and six Ameri- cans reported killed and 21 wounded. Only scattered fighting has been reported else- where in South Vietnam. Units of the 18-battalion allied Operation ATTLEBORO sweeping central Tay Ninh Province near the Communists' War Zone "C" report no significant contact with the enemy. Sev- eral additional enemy food and munitions caches have been discovered, however. Y Bham is now demanding virtually complete control of all the montagnard peoples under a separate administration with its own army, police, and educa- tional and judicial systems.
