Jesus' words, "Ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free," came to him with particular force, he recalls, on that bit- terly cold night in February 1968 when a carload of armed soldiers took him from his wife and four sleeping children and clanged him into prison. It could have been a moment of shattering defeat. All he had believed in and worked for seemed to have been negated. Mr. Rittenberg had to get back to fundamentals. What made life worth living? Wife, children, friends, books, music, food? All these he loved.
