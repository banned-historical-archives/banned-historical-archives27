(c) The policy of forming a national United'Front must be considered an attack on the other parties and deadly blows to Dutch imperial- .ism. (2) Economy (a) (b) Nationalization of key industries must be extended by making that policy a program of the people, the workers, and the peasants. Divisi6n of feudal land holdings and land held by the imperialists must tsk place according to the will of the people. (3) Labor (a) The Sentral Organisasi Buruh Seluruh Indonesia (SOBSI), which has much influence already, must be strengthened by PKI participation in SOBSI. (b) Labor unions led by reactionaries must be discarded by SOBSI and the PKI by isolating them from the masses. (c) To insure that the tactic of driving out the reactionaries does not fail, the policy of a United Labor Front must be accomplished on a mass basis and not by the leaders only. (d) The Sentral Organisasi Buruh Republik Indonesia (SOBRI), however, must be cracked because it is a dangerous Trotskyite element. Its activity must therefore be carefully countered. (4) Peasants (a) Because the peasants form 70 percent of the population, their power must be mobilized by the PKI through the Barisan Tani Indonesia (BTI), Sarekat Buruh Perkebunan Republik Indonesia(5ARBUMI) and the Sarekat Buruh Gula (SBG). (b) The formation of peasants' organizations must be done in every Section Committee of the PKI.
