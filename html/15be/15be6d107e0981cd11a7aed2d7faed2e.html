(Organizations whose leading officials have not been identified since December 1980 have been excluded from this directory.) The early and late dates denote specific identification of an individual in his position. The names of females are followed by the letter f Also included are a table of contents and indexes to organizations and personalities (the latter with Standard Telegraphic Codes when available). This publication supersedes the December 1982 Directory of Chinese Officials: Scientific and Educational Organizations (CR 82-16310). It is intended to complement the National-Level Organizations volume of the Directory of Chinese Officials series. (The third volume in the series is Provincial Organizations.) The orthography adopted is the Pinyin system for the romanization of Chinese characters.
