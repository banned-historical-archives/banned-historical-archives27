Preface Relations between the nations of the world are increasingly close and interdependent as a result of the speed of modern communications. With this situation in mind, a group of men desirous of pro- moting international understanding and human fraternity have combined their physical, mental, and material resources and founded the Maitri San, which is designed to serve as a good- will message from the Chinese to their brothers, the Thai. The Witri San aims at the dissemination of news rather than propa- ganda and will try to avoid all political bias. We wish to stress the fact that our aim is not political, as in the case of British US, and Soviet publicity, and that we are not connected with any other persons or organization.
