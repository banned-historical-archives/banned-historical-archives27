A recent recommenda- tion of the EEC Commission makes the EEC position uncertain. Under the terms of this new proposal, cuts in tariffs among EEC member states sched- uled for this July would be doubled to 20 percent to satis- fy desires of the six, par- ticularly of France,for accel- erated consolidation of the EEC as a distinctive unit.
