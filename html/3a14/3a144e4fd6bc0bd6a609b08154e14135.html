The Chinese used the occasion to raise their commitment to the PRG as the "sole authentic representative" of the South Vietnamese people. After maintaining almost total silence on the Kissinger-Le Duc Tho Paris talks, Hanoi media or. 13 June carried the announcement that agreement had been reached on communiques to be signed by the United States and the DRV and by the four parties to the Vietnamese agreements, respectively.
