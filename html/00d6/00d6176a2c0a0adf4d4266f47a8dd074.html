Peiping has been out to make its decennial celebration tomorrow a great "spectacular" of Chinese Communist strength and achieve- ment. A. The USSR and the Asian satellites are sending their top drawer Communists. B. But the Chinese may not be entirely gratified by the response from the rest of the bloc. The East Europeans are mostly sending delegations headed by titular heads of state. Only Albania and Czechoslovakia are sending top political figures. C. Khrushchev is probably going more to discuss vital Sino-Soviet issues than to lend his prestige to the ceremonies. 1. Chinese have been reserved about merits of Khrushchev visit to United States. Khrushchev will probably give his account of visit and reassure Peiping that Chinese interests have not been compromised. 2. Soviet officials, for their part, have been less than enthusiastic over Peiping's handling of Laotian crisis and border dispute with India. 3. Both sides have differed on merits of Chinese commune and "leap forward" efforts. 4. We can expect real effort to smooth over differences and joint communique affirming bloc solidarity. III. Attendance of Chinese officials at ceremonies will provide fresh insights into government shakeup announced by Peiping two weeks ago. A. Highlight of shakeup was dismissal of Defense Minister Peng Te-huai. 1. Peng apparently still retains his other titles (vice premier, politburo member) but he hasn't been seen or heard of since 21 June. 2. Cause of his dismissal not announced. There have been indications, however, that some military people were at odds with the party over issues such as party control over army, regime's hot-and-cold tactics in Taiwan Strait, enforced participation of army in agriculture and other economic activities. 3. Selection of Minister of Public Security as new chief of staff augurs for increased party surveillance over army. B. Dismissal of two senior deputy foreign ministers had anti- Soviet overtone. 1. Both were former ambassadors to Moscow with record of affinity for Soviet viewpoints. 2. Mao may have been exasperated by their harping on Soviet examples in face of Chinese commune and leap forward innovations.
