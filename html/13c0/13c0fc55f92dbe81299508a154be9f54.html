Foreign Policy Implications Kaganovich reaffirmed the USSR's loyalty to "the struggle for peace and coexistence" but reiterated the adamant Soviet stand on the German issue and the need for agreeing on a European security plan. His brief reference to disarmament marked time along the lines of Bulganin's 19 September letter to President Eisenhower in stressing the necessity of end- ing the armaments race and ban- ning atomic weapons.
