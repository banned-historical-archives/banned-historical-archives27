He said that political parties had greatly influenced the development of Indonesian worker organizations, which, in turn, reflected the dif- ferences between the parties. He asset~ted that the Indonesian worker should have one philosophy and one objective of struggle. Other indicators in this consolidation trend by the government have been the unification into one group of all veterans organizations and the call by President Sukarno for all youth organizations to unite. The government has already taken steps to simplify the political party system, and there are indications that the government also intends to simplify the 'system of functional groups, such as the workers, veterans, and youth, all of which are to constitute the new National Front, to be represented In the new Parliament.
