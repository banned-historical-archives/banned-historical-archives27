The troublesome quotation, which first appeared in a People's Daily editorial on February 9th, was expanded on in a series of articles carried in official party papers and journals. Besides calling for stricter adherence to party discipline and for an end to public disturbances, the articles continue the strong attack on a number of common practices that undermine the collectivized economy.
